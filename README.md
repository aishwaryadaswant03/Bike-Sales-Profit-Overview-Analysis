Bike Sales Dashboard (2013â€“2014) analyzes sales, profit, discounts, and costs across products, segments, and regions, helping identify top performers, margin gaps, and opportunities to improve profitability through data-driven decisions.

**Objectives**

Evaluate bike sales and profit performance,
Identify impact of discounts and costs on margins,
Support data-driven pricing and sales decisions.

**Tools & Technologies**

Power BI,
DAX,
Excel for importing data.

**Key KPIs & Metrics**

Sales, Gross Sales, Net Sales
Profit & COGS,
Units Sold,
Discount Amount,
Average Sale Price (ASP).

**Key Insights**

Sales and profit increased from 2013 to 2014,
High discounts reduced margins in some segments,
Paseo was the top-performing product,
Profitability varied across regions and segments.

**Business Recommendations**

Control discounts to protect margins
Improve pricing in low-profit regions
Focus on high-performing products
Optimize costs to improve profitability
